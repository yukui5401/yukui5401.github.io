Hi. I'm Brooke (he/him), an undergraduate studying Combinatorics & Optimization (Pure Math Joint) at the University of Waterloo.

This site hosts my math blog, where I write about the ideas I find most elegant or surprising — with a soft spot for anything combinatorial. I believe math underlies more of our reality than most people might realize, and that sharing these discoveries is a way to contribute to something bigger than my own curiosity.
